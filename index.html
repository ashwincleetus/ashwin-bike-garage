Our Story

Built on Passion,
Driven by Precision

Ashwin Bike Garage was born from a lifelong obsession with motorcycles. What started as a small workshop in kanyakumari has grown into one of the city's most trusted destinations for bikers — from daily commuters to weekend warriors.

Every bike that rolls through our doors gets the same treatment: honest diagnosis, quality parts, and workmanship we stand behind. No shortcuts. No upselling. Just straight talk and solid work.
